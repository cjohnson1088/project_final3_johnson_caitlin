Welcome to Golden Donut

of Columbus, GA

We are a locally-owned donut manufacturer serving retail customers in Columbus and surrounding areas. We also manufacture and wholesale donuts to customers throughout the Southeast, Midwest, Puerto Rico, and Southeast Asia.

At our facility at 625 Manchester Expressway, you can come inside or visit our convenient drive-thru window to purchase hot, fresh donuts. You can call ahead so we can have them ready for pick-up. As your fundraising headquarters, we can help you raise money for your organization.

For our wholesale customers, we can quote delivered or pick-up pricing at our plant. Please explore our website for more information about our products.

Come see us anytime - we're open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
